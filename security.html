Security & Protection

Comprehensive CCTV

At Noda hotel, we take the security and protection of our guests very seriously. That's why we have implemented a comprehensive CCTV system throughout the property to ensure the safety and well-being of all our guests. Our CCTV cameras are strategically placed to provide optimal coverage and are constantly monitored by our security team to ensure the immediate response to any potential threat. We believe that your peace of mind is of the utmost importance, and we are committed to providing you with a secure and comfortable stay. Please do not hesitate to reach out to our front desk or security team if you have any concerns or questions regarding our CCTV system.

Highly Trained Security Personnel

At our hotel, we take our guests' safety and protection extremely seriously. We have a group of highly skilled security people on-site to assure your safety and wellbeing because of this. Our security team is on call around-the-clock to help with any worries or problems that could come up. To ensure the security of all visitors and to immediately address any potential threats, they are continually keeping an eye on the premises. We are dedicated to giving you a safe and enjoyable stay since we think your peace of mind is extremely important. Please do not hesitate to contact our security staff or front desk if you require assistance or have any questions or concerns. They'll be delighted to help you.

Keycard System

At Noda hotel, we prioritize the security and protection of our guests. To ensure this, we have implemented a keycard system for all guest rooms. This system provides you with a personalized keycard that is required for entry into your room, ensuring that only authorized individuals have access to it and providing an extra layer of security for your belongings. The keycard system also eliminates the need for traditional keys, which can be easily copied or lost. We are committed to providing our guests with the highest level of security and protection, and we hope that you find this system convenient and secure. If you have any questions or concerns, please don't hesitate to contact our front desk.
